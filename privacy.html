Privacy Policy

Mnac app · Last updated: June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Mnac app handles your data.

Data Collection

Mnac does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not gather your name, email address, location, or any other personal information.

Data Storage

All data you enter (such as your service dates) is stored exclusively on your device. This data is never sent to any server and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

Internet Connection

The app works fully offline. An internet connection is not required and is not used to transmit any data.

Third Parties

Mnac does not use any third-party analytics services, advertising, or tracking tools.

Data Deletion

Since all data is stored on your device, you can delete it simply by removing the app from your device.

Contact
